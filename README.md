# Electrical Power Distribution System Design for Facility in Lethbridge, AB

This repository contains the comprehensive electrical design and power system analysis for a multipurpose industrial/commercial facility in near Lethbridge, AB
## Project Overview

The project focuses on the detailed electrical infrastructure for a 10-acre site featuring 5 building plans. The design prioritizes safety, efficiency, and sustainability by integrating renewable energy and advanced protection systems.

### Technical Highlights
* **Primary Supply:** 25 kV Delta utility feeder.
* **Secondary System:** 112.5 kVA, 208 V, 3-phase Star connection.
* **Emergency Systems:** 250 kW Kohler Diesel Generator & 25 kVA Schneider Electric UPS.
* **Sustainability:** 176 kW Solar PV system (352 panels) reducing ~164.75 metric tons of CO2 annually.

## Project Methodology

The design followed a rigorous process flow to ensure compliance with Canadian Electrical Code (CEC) standards:
1. **Load Analysis:** Comprehensive calculation of lighting (Lumen) and receptacle loads.
2. **System Design:** Creating detailed power and lighting layouts.
3. **Modeling:** Developing Single-Line Diagrams (SLD) for normal and emergency operations.
4. **Power Analysis:** Utilizing ETAP for Load Flow, Short Circuit, and Arc Flash studies.
5. **Protection Coordination:** Implementation of Siemens 7SR11 relays and Vacuum Circuit Breakers (VCB).

* **AutoCAD:** Technical drafting for power and electrical plans.
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data organization and schedule management.
